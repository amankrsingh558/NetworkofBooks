FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)                   






Q. How do I borrow books

        You can chat with the owner of the book and then make your adjustment.

Q. Do I have to pay money

       May be or may not be. Depends on the Owner.

Q. How to get books if owner resides far from me.

       You can filter your books according to the city and find nearest person near you.

Q. What if the Book is lost

       You have to either replace the material or pay 1.5x the price of the book
